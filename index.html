Hi, there ! My name is Kirsty. I initially studied to be a product and industrial designer at Central Saint Martins, developing a keen interest in UX design and design thinking as innovation. However, after completing a diploma in creative computing at the Creative Computing Institute, I realised I was passionate about technology and I fell in love with coding, so much that I decided I wanted to do it full-time!

Currently I am now a full-stack software developer, having just graduated from the Tech Accelerator Programme at _nology . In my spare time I like to explore the intersection of AI, machine learning, design and creativity, and learning about AI/future ethics. Sometimes you can find me DJing at events and the odd radio show, selecting tunes and curating sets to make people dance!
